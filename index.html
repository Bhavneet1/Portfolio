Who We Are

escoADVISOR is a globally recognized software, consulting and EDi services leader that provides digital solutions to energy service companies(ESCOs), utilities, and energy enterprises worldwide. We offer a proven and cutting-edge, comprehensive Billing/CIS as well as EDI consulting services to our clients. We are a company deeply rooted in the retail energy industry, and our experienced, professional leadership and staff ensure that whether you are an Energy Supplier, Utility, Energy Broker/Aggregator or Self Service client, our products shall provide you with the edge to succeed in today’s competitive energy marketplace.

Our sole focus is EDi transactions and we develop, market, and support a single secure cloud based well planned suite of products. Our heritage is rooted in doing what is best for our customers. We take pride in our long dedicated customer service and remain committed to our continuing objective of being a better EDi service company.
