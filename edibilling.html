AR Aging issue

AR aging is used to track status of customer’s payments and identify problems where customers did not pay on time or payments not associated at account level.

escoADVISOR help ESCOs in resolving AR aging issue by identifying delinquent accounts and analyze the 820 payment Edi files with corresponding meter readings i.e. 867 and invoice i.e. 810 created by Edi vendor. We have cloud based APIs {Application Programming Interface} solution that can integrate with EDi service provider and generate the error. At the same time, we do check at account level balance error to give correct resolution to an ESCO.

General AR aging problems:

escoADVISOR categorize the AR aging issue on the basis of utilities served and billing method against each customer.

1. Bill Ready customers AR aging issue:

Just looking into current balance for 30, 60, 90 days etc. under AR aging is NOT enough to say customer did not pay since that many days. To resolve the issue ESCO have to perform at least below steps to make sure the outstanding balance is correct.

  • Check 867 meter reading transactions for the account. Also, make sure we are reading correct Usage tag within the Edi file.
  • Check corresponding 810 invoices generated for the meter readings received.
  • Check cancel meter reading transactions created i.e. credit memo for the account.
  • During invoice creation process, check if cancel and original meter reading generated one invoice or separate invoices. Each utility behave differently for cancel meter reading invoices.
  • Check if the invoice got accepted or rejected i.e. 824 application advice file.
  • Check if 820 payment file processed within the Edi service provider and payment got posted correctly.
  • Check for dates discrepancies between Utility and ESCO level
  • Check for Master accounts fees i.e. Bill Fee and Credit and Collections fee.
  • Check for duplicate 810 created for 867`s.
  • Check for manual 820 payment file processed; for example in OH territory, we have to create 820 payment file on the basis of invoice i.e. 810.
  • Data migration issue during Edi service provider switch.
  • For PSEG; conversion of account change request from bill ready to DUAL and initiated by utility.
  • Many more…

escoADVISOR designed and developed cloud based APIs, that look into every possible scenarios and give the right result for every account. Also, we do check at account level before coming up with any resolution for AR aging accounts.

2. Rate Ready customers AR aging issue:

As we do NOT generate/create invoices i.e. 810 for rate ready customers; we have to make sure the shadow invoices created at ESCO level is matching with corresponding 820 payment file.

  • Check 867 {Meter Readings} and 810 {Invoices} processed correctly into billing system. For example: Utility i.e. coned send 867 and 810 against each payment received and every 867 has corresponding 810 transactions ID. If for any reason meter reading did not receive corresponding inbound invoice will cause outstanding balance and account will show under AR aging.
  • Master Account transactions where utility is taking the money out account from ESCO. Fee like administrative fee, C& C fee etc.
  • Check to make sure inbound 810 file had rate and tax information and matching with 820 file.
  • OH territory; for some utilities we received payment manually send to the ESCO and ESCO is responsible to process within the billing system.
  • CL&P; Excel file directly sent to the ESCO.
  • Cancel meter reading causing edi remittance adjustment and not able to associate with the cancel invoice created within billing software.
  • Many more…

escoADVISOR Edi experts look into every possible scenarios against rate ready utilities and give the correct result for every account. Also, we do check at account level before coming up with any resolution for AR aging accounts.

3. DUAL customers AR aging issue:

To resolve AR aging issue for DUAL accounts, need to analyze below points to resolve the issue

  • 814 change request initiated from bill ready or rate ready method to DUAL so payment file for a specific period came as 820 payment instead of posting payment at account level.
  • Check 867 meter reading transactions for the account. Also, make sure we are reading correct Usage tag within the Edi file.
  • Check corresponding 810 invoices generated for the meter readings received.
  • Check cancel meter reading transactions created i.e. credit memo for the account.
  • During invoice creation process, check if cancel and original meter reading generated one invoice or separate invoices. Each utility behave differently for cancel meter reading invoices.
  • Check if the invoice got accepted or rejected i.e. 824 application advice file.
  • Check if 820 payment file processed within the Edi service provider and payment got posted correctly.
  • Check for dates discrepancies between Utility and ESCO level.
  • Check for Master accounts fees i.e. Bill Fee and Credit and Collections fee.
  • Check for duplicate 810 created for 867`s.
  • Check for manual 820 payment file processed; for example in OH territory, we have to create 820 payment file on the basis of invoice i.e. 810.
  • Data migration issue during Edi service provider switch.
  • For PSEG; conversion of account change request from bill ready to DUAL and initiated by utility.
  • Many more…

4. Marketer Bill Ready AR aging issues:

escoADVISOR designed and developed cloud based APIs, that look into every possible scenarios for marketer bill ready customer and give the right AR aging data for every account. Also, we do check at account level before coming up with any resolution for AR aging accounts.

escoADVISOR team through understanding below inbound and outbound transactions that help in resolving issues before happening ESCO customers.

  • 810 invoice.
  • 824 application advice.

In NY, NJ, OH, TX, PA etc. territory common rate, tax, enrollment, auto bill issues are:
